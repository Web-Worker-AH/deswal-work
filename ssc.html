STAFF SELECTION COMMISSION

EXAMS CONDUCTED BY SSC

CHSL

COMBINED HIGHER SECONDARY LEVEL(CHSL) is the exam conducted by staff selection commision(ssc) .This Exam is conducted for the different post of Grade C . DEO(Data Entry Operator),LDC(Lower Division Clerk). Huge number of candidates apply for this exam and the competition is tough. 12th pass is the eligibility for these posts. The post of Data operator undertake the job of processing data in government offices whereas the LDC have the job functions which include registration,maintenance,indexing and recording,typing, comparing and preparation of various statements .

CGL

COMBINED GRADUATE LEVEL(CGL) is the exam conducted by staff selection commision(ssc).Graduation from any recognised University is the minimum educational qualification .This exam is conducted for different central civil posts -Assistant/Inspector/ SI/Divisional Accountant/Junior Accountant/UDC/Tax Assistant/compiler.Selected candidates get jobs in departments of Central Secretariat Service/Intelligence Bureau/Ministry Of Railways /Ministry Of External Affairs/Ministry Of Defence/CBI and many other government services.
