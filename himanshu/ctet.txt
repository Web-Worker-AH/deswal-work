<h1 class="sub_headings">ELIGIBILITY FOR CTET</h1>
<p>The candidates having the following minimum educational qualification are eligible for appearing in CTET</p>
<h1 class="sub_headings">
Minimum Educational Qualification for becoming Teacher for Classes I to V 
(primary stage):
</h1>
<p>Senior Secondary(or its equivalent)with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 
2-year Diploma in Elementary Education(by whatever known)
<p> or</p>
</p>
<p>Senior Secondary(or its equivalent)with at least 45% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 
2-year Diploma in Eementary Education (by whatever known),in accordance with NCTE 
(Recognition Norms and Procedure),Regulations,2002.
</p>
<p> or</p>
<p>Senior Secondary(or its equivalent)with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 
4-year Bachelor of Eementary Education(B.EL.ED).
<p> or</p>
<p>Senior Secondary(or its equivalent)with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year of 
2-year Diploma in Education(Special education)*.</p>
<p> or</p>
<p>Graduation and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education(by whatever name known).
</p>

<h1 class="sub_headings">

Minimum Educational Qualification 
for becoming Teacher for Classes VI to VIII(elementary stage):
</h1>
<p>
Graduation and passed or appearing in final year of 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education(by whatever name known).
</p>
<p> or</p>
<p>
Graduation with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in 1-year Bachelor in Education(B.ED).
</p>
<p> or</p>
<p>Graduation with at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in 1-year Bachelor in Education(B.ED) in accordance
 with the NCTE(Recognition Norms and Procedure) Regulations Issued from time to time in this regard.</p>
<p> or</p>
<p>
senior seconadary (or its equivalent) with  at least 50% marks and passed or appearing in final year 
of 4-year Bachelor in Elementary Education (B.EL.ED).
</p>
<h4 class="sub_headings">Note:</h4>
<p>Relaxation up to 5% inthe qualifying marks in the minimum Educational Qualification for eligibility shall 
be allowed to the candidates belonging to reserved categories,such as ST/SC/OBC/Differently abled.
</p>
